Episode 5: Patterns and Practices

The biggest public corruption investigation in Illinois history continues. Madigan says he did nothing wrong, but he is out of politics and his political machine destroyed. Has the game changed, or do Springfield’s new power players still follow the Madigan Rule?

In the fifth installment of the BGA’s limited series podcast “The Madigan Rule,” host Justin Kaufmann dives into the investigation, the resignations and the end of the Madigan era with Representative La Shawn Ford, former State Senator Heather Steans, ProPublica reporter Mick Dumke, BGA's David Kidwell, WBEZ statehouse reporter Tony Arnold, Chicago Tribune Reporter Ray Long, Illinois Public Radio reporter Hanna Meisel, former Illinois Governor Jim Edgar and former Illinois Republican Party Chairman Pat Brady.
